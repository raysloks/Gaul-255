Rogue

Level 1

Sneak Attack

Your flanking bonus on attacks is increased to 1d4.

Voracity

When one of your dice explodes, you may spend one mana to add another 1d4 to the roll.

Level 2

Blade Ward

Action - gain 1d4 damage reduction until the start of your next turn.

Slice and Dice

Spend one mana to cause the next attack that explodes to grant you a free attack action per explosion.

Level 3

Backstab

Attacks you make against a flanked or unaware enemy that explode deal an additional 1d4 damage.

Thriller

Regain one hit point per explosion whenever one of your attacks explode.

Level 4

Evasion

Gain damage reduction against area of effects equal to your rogue level.

Fan of Knives

Action - spend one mana to make an attack against a number of targets equal to your rogue level within 2 units.
