Monk

Level 1

Flurry of Blows

After making an attack, you may spend a point of energy to make another attack against the same target.

Meditate

When using the rest action, you may reroll any dice lower than your Wisdom.

Level 2

Combo Strikes

Attacks made against a target gain a damage bonus equal to the number of previous consecutive successful attacks.
The combo is broken if you miss an attack or if you, at the start of your turn, haven't made an attack since the start of your last turn.

Discipline

When you score a critical strike, you may reroll the attack to regain a point of energy.

Level 3

Interval Training

After taking the rest action, all successful attacks until the end of your turn restore one hit point.

Flow

You may take an additional action on your turn if you did not do so during your last turn.

Level 4

Peace of Mind

The rest action restores an additional 1d6 hit points. Another 1d6 is added at level 7, 10, 13, etc.

Combo Mastery

Once per turn you may reroll a die that is lower than your number of combo points.

Level 5

Fighting Style

You gain one of the following fighting styles.

Bear

Spending a point of energy increases your Strength by one.
These extra points of Strength are lost one by one at the start of your turns.
Also grants Maul.

Maul

You may spend a point of energy to increase the damage of an attack by your Strength.

Crane

You may spend one point of energy to immediately return to the position you where in at the start of your turn.
Also grants Spinning Crane Kick.

Spinning Crane Kick

You may pay one point of energy to make an attack against all targets within one unit.
The attacks must be made in either clockwise or counterclockwise order.

Monkey

When using the dodge reaction to reroll an attack, the attacker rolls with disadvantage.
You also gain Evasion.

Evasion

You gain advantage on saves made to avoid an area of effect.

Tiger

Your critical strike range is increased by one. Scoring a critical strike allows you to make another attack.

Turtle

Successful attacks restore one hit point. Interval Training is replaced by Turtle Shell.

Turtle Shell

After taking the rest action, you gain a number of temporary hit points equal to your monk level until the start of your next turn.

Level ?

Improved Fighting Style

Tiger

Your critical strike range is increased by one. Your critical strike modifier is increased by one.

Turtle

After taking the rest action, the next attack that hits you has its damage halved. If the attack is a critical strike, you regain one point of energy.
