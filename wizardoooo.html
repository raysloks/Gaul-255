You know a number of spells equal to your Intelligence. You gain a bonus to Arcana equal to your wizard level. By spending a number of hours and mana, you may transcribe a spell onto an empty scroll. By spending one mana and an hour, you may prepare a number of spells at any level, so that the total level is smaller or equal to your wizard level. Once per long rest, when you have no mana you may spend an action to regain 1d4 mana. Select one spell to master, the mana cost of that spell is reduced by one.
