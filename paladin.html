Paladin

Level 1

Smite

You can spend one mana when attacking an enemy to increase the damage dealt by 1d6.
If the target is undead, an aberration, a spirit, or an extraplanar entity, the damage is increased by an additional 1d6.

Lay on Hands

When using the rest action, you may restore hit points to another creature within one unit instead.

Level 2

Resolve

You gain a bonus to Constitution saving throws equal to half your paladin level, rounded down.

Miracle Worker

You may pay one mana to make a rest action heal wounds aswell.
