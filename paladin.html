Paladin

Level 1

Smite

You can spend one mana when attacking an enemy to double the damage dealt.
If the target is undead, an aberration, a spirit, or an extraplanar entity, you need not spend any mana.

Lay on Hands

When using the rest action, you may restore hit points to another creature within one unit instead.
Additionally, the rest action restores an additional 1d6 hit points.

Level 2

Resolve

You may spend one mana to gain resistance against one effect.

Miracle Worker

When using the rest action, you may spend one mana to make it restore total hit points aswell.
