Minor Feats

Adrenaline Rush

Gain a point of exhaustion to increase all your physical stats by 1d4 for 1 round.

Aimed Shot

Spend an action to take aim at a target, gaining advantage on the next ranged attack you make against the target before the end of the next turn.

Alert

Gain a +5 bonus to initiative.

All Out

You may, on your turn, spend a reaction to take an additional action with disadvantage.

Bullheaded

Gain a +3 bonus when charging or bull rushing.

Feint

When making a melee attack, you may have an Intelligence contest with the target. If successful you gain a +5 bonus on the attack. Otherwise, you provoke an attack of opportunity from the target.

Fleet

Gain 1 movement.

Intrepid

Gain 2 movement when moving towards an enemy.

Malice

You may gain a point of exhaustion to increase all damage you deal until the beginning of your next turn by 4.

Power Attack

When making a melee attack, you may elect to make the attack powerful, dealing an extra 1d6 damage but having a -4 penalty to hit.

Reckless Strike

When making a melee attack, you may elect to make the attack reckless, dealing an extra 1d6 damage but provoking attacks of opportunity.

Quillbearer

Once per long rest, you can choose to have advantage on an Intelligence-based skill check.

Stout

Gain a +5 bonus to all Constitution checks.

Stunning Palm

Pay 1 hit point to make an unarmed melee attack, stunning the target for 1 round if successful. A critical hit stuns the target for 1d4 rounds.

Torment

Successfully striking a target 2 turns in a row causes the next attack to hit them to deal an additional 1d6 damage.

Wildling

Gain a +3 bonus on all Survival checks.
