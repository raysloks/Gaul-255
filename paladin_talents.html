Paladin Talents

Level 1

Shield of Faith

Your shield gains one additional armor.

Sword of Faith

Your attacks deal one additional damage.

Level 2

Sundering Smite

Smiting a target reduces their armor by one.

Thundering Smite

Smiting a target deals an additional 1d6 thunder damage to the target and all enemies within 2 units of it.

Stunning Smite

Smiting a target stuns it for one turn.

Level 3

Level 4

Crusader Strike

If Smite reduces its target to zero hit points, you regain the mana spent on the Smite.

Eyes of the Inquisition

You may pay one mana to gain truesight within 10 units until the end of your next turn.

Level 5

Inspired Charge

Before using Smite, you may take the move action for free.
