Stats

Hit Points

Your maximum number of hit points is equal to your Constititution plus your level.

Sanity Points

Your maximum number of sanity points is equal to your Wisdom plus your level.

Movement

Your base movement is equal to 4.

Skills

You have a number of skill points equal to your Intelligence plus your level.

Combat

Actions

You may take three actions per round of combat.

Attack

Make an attack with one of your weapons against a target within range.

Melee

You roll your weapon's damage dice, exploding. This is your attack score. The target then chooses one of the following ways to generate their defense score.

Parry

The target chooses one of their weapons to parry your attack with. The target rolls that weapon's damage dice. This is the target's defense score. If the target's defense score is less than your attack score, the target is dealt damage equal to the difference. If the target's defense score is greater than your attack score, you are dealt damage equal to the difference.

Dodge

If the attack is an attack of opportunity, the target must choose this reaction. If not, the target must be able to use the Move action to choose this reaction.

The target's defense score is equal to their Agility. If the target's defense score is less than your attack score, the target is dealt damage equal to the difference. If the attack was not an attack of opportunity, the target must then use the Move action. It may do so without provoking an attack of opportunity from you.

Block

The target chooses one of their shields to block your attack with. Your attack score is dealt as damage to that shield.

Ranged

You roll your weapon's damage dice, exploding. This is your attack score. Reduce your attack score based on the distance between you and your target and your weapon's ranges.

Move

Move a number of meters less than or equal to your movement. If you are threatened by more than one creature, you provoke an attack of opportunity from all creatures threatening you. If you leave a creature's threat range, you provoke an attack of opportunity from that creature. You can only provoke one attack of opportunity from each creature per Move action.

Rest

Regain 1 hit point.
