Stats

Hit Points

Your maximum number of hit points is equal to your Constititution plus your level.

Sanity Points

Your maximum number of sanity points is equal to your Wisdom plus your level.

Movement

Your base movement is equal to 4.

Skills

You have a number of skill points equal to your Intelligence plus your level.

Combat

Actions

You may take three actions per round of combat.

Attack

Make an attack with one of your weapons against a target within range.

Critical Damage

When dealing critical damage, roll twice the number of dice. Any dice that come up as their maximum number explode, adding and rolling another die of the same type. Dice smaller than a d4 cannot explode.

Melee

You roll a d20, adding your Strength, Dexterity, and weapon score. This is your attack score. If the score is larger than the target's defense score, you deal damage to the target. If the score is smaller than or equal to the target's defense score, the target deals damage to you. If the rolled number is 20, you automatically deal critical damage to the target. If the rolled number is 1, the target automatically deals critical damage to you.

Parry

The target chooses one of their weapons to parry your attack with. Their defense score is equal to 10 plus their Strength, Dexterity, and weapon score.

Dodge

If the attack is an attack of opportunity, the target must choose this reaction. If not, the target must be able to use the Move action to choose this reaction.

The target chooses to dodge the attack. Their defense score is equal to 10 plus their Agility and their movement. If the attack was not an attack of opportunity, the target must then use the Move action, moving at least one space. It may do so without provoking an attack of opportunity from you.

Block

The target chooses one of their shields to block your attack with. Their defense score is equal to 10 plus their Strength, Dexterity, and weapon score, but discounting the bonus from the shield. If your attack score is larger than the target's defense score, you deal damage to the shield. If the rolled number is 20, you may choose to either deal critical damage to the shield, or deal damage to the target. If the rolled number is 1, the target deals damage to you.

Ranged

Move

Move a number of spaces less than or equal to your movement. If you are threatened by more than one creature, you provoke an attack of opportunity from all creatures threatening you. If you leave a creature's threat range, you provoke an attack of opportunity from that creature. You can only provoke one attack of opportunity from each creature per Move action.

Rest

Regain 1 hit point.
