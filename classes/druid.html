Druid

Attack bonus is one every third level.

Level 1

Druidic Spellcasting

Druids use the power of nature to cast spells, the very trees and stones bending to their will.

Draw Power - I

Action - You draw power from the land itself. Choose a number of power tokens to draw from the environment pool.

Action - Choose a spell, aswell as a number of power tokens in your possession. Use those tokens to cast the spell. If the tokens do not match the spell's requirements, the spell may fizzle or be changed.

If, at the end of your turn, you have more power tokens than your level plus 2, you must return a number of tokens equal to the excess.

Cleanse - I

Action - Purifying your body and soul, you return or destroy all power tokens in your possession.

Legends

Deep Soul

The number of power tokens you can hold increases by 1 every other level, starting at 1.

Nature's Protection

Damage taken is reduced by 1 every other level, starting at 1.

Life Channeler

The range at which you can draw power from living creatures increases by 1 for each level.

Talents

Ley Lines

Action - Draw power from the cosmos, gaining a cosmic power token for each nearby ley line.

Sense Life

You are always aware of any lifeforms within 15 units.

Spirit Walker

When you gain one or more Rot tokens, you may immediately destroy one of them without triggering its effect.

Draw Heat

Action - Choose a number of Fire tokens to gain, adding the same number of Cold tokens to the pool.

Draw Cold

Action - Choose a number of Cold tokens to gain, adding the same number of Fire tokens to the pool.

Level 2

Talents

Lodestone

Your movement is reduced by 2, but once per turn you may gain an Earth token.

Inner Strength

When drawing power from yourself, the number of Death tokens you gain is reduced by 1.
