Barbarian

Level 1

Rage

Dealing or taking damage grants you 1 point of fury. While you have 1 or more points of fury, you are considered raging. While raging, damage dealt is increased by 1.

Legends

Living Weapon

While raging, damage dealt is increased by 1 additional point for every other point of fury, up to a maximum of your level.

Feel No Pain

While raging, you gain 1 damage reduction for every other point of fury, up to a maximum of your level.

Vitality

Gain an additional hit point per level.
