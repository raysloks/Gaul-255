Barbarian

Class attack bonus is 4.

Level 1

Rage

Dealing or taking damage grants you 1 point of fury. While you have 1 or more points of fury, you are considered raging. While raging, damage dealt is increased by 1.

Legends

Brutal Strikes

The level of wounds you afflict with melee attacks are increased by 1d4. Changes to a d6 at level 3; a d8 at level 5; a d10 at level 7; a d12 at level 9.

Living Weapon

You gain an additional 1 attack bonus every level to attacks you make, but gain 1 negative armor every other level.

Feel No Pain

While raging, you gain 1 damage reduction for every third point of fury, up to a maximum of your level.

Vitality

Gain an additional hit point per level.

Inner Fire

Sacrifice 3 hit points to gain 1d4 points of fury. At level 3 you may also use 4 hit points to gain 1d6 fury. At 5, 5 for 1d8; 7, 6 for 1d10; 9, 7 for 1d12.

Talents

Level 2

Talents

Raging Regeneration

Spend 5 points of fury to regenerate 1d4 hit points.

Level 3

Legends

Bloodlust

When dealing damage with a melee attack, heal a number of hit points equal to the lowest of the damage dealt and your level.

Bloodboil

By spending 8 points of fury, you may take an additional action. The cost is reduced by 1 every other level, starting at 5, down to a minimum of 4.

Bloodstrike

Spend 5 points of fury to enhance an attack, you heal 1 hit point for every point of damage the attack inflicts.

Talents

Final Strike

Action - Spend all your fury to make a melee attack against a target. Gain an attack bonus and a damage bonus equal to the number of fury points spent. You may not gain fury for the rest of the round.
