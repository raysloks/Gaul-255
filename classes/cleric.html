Cleric

Class attack bonus is 2.

Level 1

Worship

Miracles

Clerics call upon the aid of gods and spirits to assist them in their endeavours.

Action - Call upon the aid of a deity.

Legends

Pious

Your favor bonus is increased by 1, but your attack bonus is reduced by 1.

Staunch

Gain 1 additional hit point every other level.

Crusader

Your attack bonus is increased by 2, but your favor bonus is reduced by 2.<

Talents

Defender of the Faith

If your current favor is higher than your current hit points, you gain 2 armor.

Shield of the Faith

If your shield is about to shatter, you may spend 1d4 favor to restore it to 1 hit point.

Faith Healer

Your Medicine skill is increased by 3.

Level 2

Faith

You have a number of faith points equal to your level. You may spend a faith point to gain 1d4 favor, but lose 1d4 at the end of the round. If you reach zero faith, you have a crisis of faith. If you succeed, half your faith points are restored. If you fail, you have disadvantage whenever you call upon a miracle until your next long rest.
