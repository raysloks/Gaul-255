Paladin

Class attack bonus is 4.

Level 1

Smite

Action - Enhance an attack to deal double damage. Must be declared before rolling for an attack. Can be used a number of times per long rest equal to your level.

Order

A paladin may elect to be a member of an order.

Legends

Holy Smite

Smite deals 1 additional damage per level, before multiplication. Smite deals double damage against undead.

Shield of Humanity

Sacrifice a use of Smite to reduce the damage dealt to you or an by an amount equal to your level. More than one Smite charge can be used at once.
