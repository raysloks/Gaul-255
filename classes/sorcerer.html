Sorcerer

Class attack bonus is 1.

Level 1

Innate Spellcasting

Action - Cast an innate spell.

Wild Magic

Whenever you roll a d20 as part of casting a spell, any fumbles trigger a wild magic surge. Criticals also trigger a wild magic surge, but you can choose whether the surge's effect takes place or not.

Legends

Vitality

Gain an additional hit point per level.

Guided Spells

Gain an additional spell attack bonus per level.

Mana Font

If you were to be reduced to zero hit points by a spell's cost, you may regain 1d4 hit points. If a 1 is rolled, this ability may not be used until you finish a long rest. The die changes to a d6 at 3rd level; a d8 at 5th level; a d10 at 7th level; a d12 at 9th level.

Talents

Chaotic Mind

You may intentionally trigger a wild magic surge.

Storm Shield

Your body crackles with magical energy, dealing 1 lightning damage to anything making a melee attack against you.

Fire Shield

Your body is surrounded by scorching heat, dealing 1 fire damage to anything making a melee attack against you.
