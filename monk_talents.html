Monk Talents

Level 1

Slow Fall

The damage taken from falling is reduced by one die per monk level.

Level 2

Stunning Palm

When making an attack, you may pay one point of energy to empower the attack.
If the attack deals final damage equal to or larger than twice the target's Constitution, the target is stunned for one turn.

Ki Healing

When using the rest action, you may restore maximum hit points to another creature within one unit instead.

Windstrike

You may pay one point of energy to increase the range of one of your attacks by 3 units.
