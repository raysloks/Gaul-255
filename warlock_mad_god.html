Warlock - Mad God

Level 1

Insanity

After a creature gains or loses insanity, they take 1d4 psychic damage for every insanity they have. When a creature takes the rest action, they lose one insanity. Gains and losses of insanity are generally applied on a per action basis.

Whispers from Below

Action - gain one insanity to make a target within 10 units gain one insanity.

Grip on Reality

Gain psychic damage reduction equal to your warlock level.

Return from the Depths

Action - spend one mana to lose 1d4 insanity.

Level 2

Shattered Mind

Dealing psychic damage to a target causes them to lose an action for every 8 damage dealt.

Beacon of Madness

Action - spend one mana to make a target within 10 units a Beacon of Madness, causing all its insanity gains to also be applied to all other targets within 10 units of it.

Level 3

Accursed Flute

Action - roll 1d4 for each insanity you have. Then, the following effects take place. 1 - for every 1 rolled, gain one insanity. 2 - for every 2 rolled, a random target within 10 units gains one insanity. 3 - for every 3 rolled, a target within 10 units gains one insanity. 4 - for every 4 rolled, all targets within 10 units gain one insanity.

Weather the Storm

You may spend one mana to gain resistance to psychic damage until the start of your next turn.
