The Infinite Staircase

Step after step, no living soul knows where it ends.

Powers

Those who climb the infinite staircase use their powers by ascending or descending a number of steps.

Level 1

Ascend - X up

Descend - X down

Level 3

Shortcut - 10 up

May only be used when your current step is a multiple of 20, not counting zero. You teleport to an air-filled location up to 10 units away.

Level 5

Window - 15 up

May only be used when your current step is a multiple of 15, not counting zero. Roll a d20. If the result is higher than 15, the GM must answer a question you ask in very vague terms. If the result is 20, the GM must answer a question you ask in very precise terms.
