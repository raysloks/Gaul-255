Sitet

The dead follow the path in which the undead colossus Sitet walks.

Powers

Followers of Sitet use their powers by gaining a number of Death power tokens.

Level 1

Bone Horror - X

Transforms a corpse into a Bone Horror with X hit points, dealing PS melee damage in range 0-2.

Level 3

Necrophage - X

Inflicts a target with a deadly disease, dealing 3 necrotic damage at end of their turn for X turns.

Level 5

Finger of Death - X

Target within 1 unit loses X hit points.

Death Nova

When you die, all creatures within 2 units take 20 necrotic damage.

Level 7

Shambling Bones - X

Transforms a quarter of X number of corpses into a level X Bone Shambler, hit points equal to its level times 4, dealing PBS melee damage in range 2-5 with cleave and an attack bonus equal to its level.

Level 9

Animate Dead - 10
